Dear Friends and Esteemed Colleagues of IOA,

I am pleased to inform you that the Hon’ble Delhi High Court has passed a significant order on 4th September 2024, resolving a long-standing matter. The main points are as follows:

"Serious irregularities have ex facie vitiated the elections held in November 2020..." (Para 64 of the judgment).

"...the new Committee will take over from the Administrator after the results of the elections, likely to be conducted in September/October 2024..." (Para 14).

It brings me immense pleasure to share that this marks the beginning of a new era for IOA, where a fresh Executive Committee will assume charge.

Key Issues to Reflect Upon

Some members had tried to delay the election process and influence the Ld. Administrator, but with the court’s order, we can now move forward with the 2024 elections.

My Appeal to You

  1. The 2020 Election was officially declared null by the Hon’ble Court.
  2. There are suspicions regarding the role of certain agencies, like CDSL, in the previous elections.
  3. We must ensure that IOA moves towards a transparent and just election process for a better future.

Let us together work to elect a clean and dynamic Executive Committee that will support members' rights.
