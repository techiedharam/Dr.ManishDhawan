I have the pleasure to inform and share with you all that the deck is now clear with the order passed by the Hon’ble Delhi High Court on 4th September 2024. The operative portions of the order are as follows:

“12. I have perused the judgment dated 20.10.2023, more particularly para 64 thereof, wherein it has been observed that serious irregularities have ex facie vitiated the elections held in November 2020. This Court had appointed the learned Administrator to supersede the Executive Committee of IOA with a direction to take appropriate steps as enumerated in para 66 of the said judgment.”

“14. Admittedly, the elections are now likely to be conducted in September/October 2024. The new Committee will take over from the Administrator after the result of said elections is announced, meaning that the Executive Committee elected in 2020, and subsequently superseded, will not be restituted. Thus, the elections for the year 2020 stand nullified for all practical purposes.”

“15. ...the contest by the plaintiff no.1 for a third time as Vice President during the 2020 elections will not be counted towards the third chance under the Code of Conduct. Accordingly, the plaintiff no.1 will be entitled to contest for Vice President in the IOA Elections 2023.”

It now gives me immense pleasure to say that with this order, we will finally have a new Executive Committee in place, without any older members. The time has come for us to be aware of our valuable rights and elect a new, clean, and dynamic EC that will support its members when needed.
