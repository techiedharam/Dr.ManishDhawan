Heartfelt Thanks and Reflections
I wish to place on record my heartfelt thanks to those who have supported and believed in my candidature for the post of Secretary General for 2023. Our struggle to cleanse IOA has been a great challenge, lasting over 4 years of upheavals. We did everything possible but may have been unable to communicate with the members about the systematic failures in IOA.
In a democracy, it is the popular vote that decides the result. I respect the decision of the IOA members and submit to it.
Now that the results have been declared, the new EC will soon take over IOA from the Administrator. Our best wishes to the incoming EC, and we hope they will be able to guide IOA out of the turmoil it has been facing.
As the Spanish saying goes, and the famous English song with the same title, Qué será, será, literally meaning "What will be, will be," we bow to the decision of the members.
As pointed out by a member a few days ago, “The government you elect is the government you deserve” (Thomas Jefferson). So be it with IOA. Let us hope for a new sunrise for the betterment.
With all the best wishes to the incoming EC.

Prof. (Dr.) Manish Dhawan
